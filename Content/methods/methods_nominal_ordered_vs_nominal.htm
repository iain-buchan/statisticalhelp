Advice: R by C Chi-square Test

 

Nominal (with order) vs. Nominal (no order)

 

r by c CHI-SQUARE TEST: This is listed under the chi-square section of the analysis menu. Arrange your data in an r by c table. The overall test investigates the null hypothesis that there is no relationship between the grouping of r and c. You must ensure that more than 80% of the expected frequencies are > 5, if they are not then you should reduce the number of categories / groups by combination or exclusion.
