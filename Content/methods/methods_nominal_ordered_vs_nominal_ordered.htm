Advice: R by C Chi-square Test for Trend

 

Nominal (with order) vs. Nominal (with order)

 

r by c CHI-SQUARE TEST FOR TREND: This is listed under the chi-square menu of the analysis menu The test looks for a trend in one group which is also reflected in the other group. You must ensure that more than 80% of the expected frequencies are > 5.
