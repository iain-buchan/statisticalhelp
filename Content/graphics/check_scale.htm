Check Scale Limits

 

If this option is checked then you will be asked to confirm the maximum and minimum scale values for the axes of most StatsDirect graphs. If this option is not checked then StatsDirect automatically sets the axis scale limits for you.

 

Please note that the scale limits you specify will not be the same as the scale limits on the final graph. The scale limits you enter are simply a target for the StatsDirect scale selection algorithm to work to.
