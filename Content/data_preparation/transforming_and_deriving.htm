Transforming and Deriving Data

 

Menu location: Data_Transforming and Deriving.

 

  • COMMON TRANSFORMS (common transforms such as logs)
  • FORMULA (apply a user-defined formula to one or more variables to derive a new one)
  • UNITS (change between different units of measurement)
  • NORMAL SCORES (calculate standard normal order statistics)
  • STANDARDIZATION (standardise variables)
  • ANTHROPOMETRIC STANDARDIZATION (growth reference z scores)
  • PAIRWISE (calculate differences, means or slopes between pairs of variables)
  • RANKS (transform variables to their ranks)
