Advice: 2 by K Chi-square Test for Trend

 

Nominal (with order) vs. Dichotomous

 

2 by k CHI-SQUARE TEST FOR TREND. This is listed under the chi-square menu of the analysis menu. The test looks for a trend in one group which is also reflected in the other group. You must ensure that more than 80% of the expected frequencies are > 5.
